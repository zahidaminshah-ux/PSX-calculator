📚 Data Sources & Assumptions
KSE-100 Returns: Total return index (dividends reinvested, adjusted for bonus & rights issues). 30-yr CAGR ≈ 14% nominal PKR (Topline Securities / Business Recorder, 2021). Recent decade (2011–2021) ≈ 14%. Bull decade (2001–2011) ≈ 25%. Pessimistic basis = bear cycle / post-MSCI demotion risk ≈ 10–12%.
Pakistan CPI Inflation: Optimistic ~7% (IMF FY26 forecast, current trajectory post-tight monetary policy). Base ~10% (10-yr weighted average including 2022–24 spike to ~38%). Pessimistic ~16% (2022–24 peak era average). Source: Pakistan Bureau of Statistics, Trading Economics, IMF WEO Oct 2025.
Withdrawal Model: Perpetual / endowment model. Corpus = Annual Withdrawal ÷ SWR. SWR = Real Return ÷ (1 + Safety Buffer). If real return > SWR, portfolio grows indefinitely. Safety buffer accounts for: sequence-of-returns risk, index tracking costs, currency devaluation beyond CPI, bad years.
Not modelled: Capital gains tax, 15% dividend withholding tax, index fund management fees (~0.5–1.5% p.a. in Pakistan), USD-denominated purchasing power erosion (PKR has depreciated ~10% per year vs USD over 20 yrs). ⚠️ This is a financial illustration only — not investment advice.
